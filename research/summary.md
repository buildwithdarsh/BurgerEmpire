## SECTION 9 — ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS

### RC-1: Legal Verdict + Silence 🔴 CRITICAL
- Permanent Google Page 1 via LiveLaw (DA 70+)
- BB denied liability in court; zero public response post-verdict
- Incident item (Veg Chilli Loaded Burger) **still on menu** despite 1,331 units sold in 2025
- Zomato de-ranking active since Apr 3, 2025

### RC-2: Zero Direct Channel Infrastructure 🟠 HIGH
- 100% aggregator dependency — no fallback when platforms break
- ONDC not registered (missed 11+ months; 3% commission vs 25%)
- WhatsApp Business ordering not active (Rs.40–50K one-time setup)
- No CRM, no loyalty programme, no direct customer database
- burgerempire.in = brochure-only, no ordering cart

### RC-3: Zomato Algorithm Demotion + Commission Squeeze 🟠 HIGH
- Post-verdict compliance breach → demotion authority from Apr 3, 2025
- Visibility Plan (Jul 2025): national chains pay Rs.6,500/month/outlet → priority riders + ranking; BB does not subscribe → sinks in search
- Effective Zomato commission: 25–35% vs ONDC's 3–8%

### RC-4: Q4 2025 Operational Collapse 🟡 MEDIUM-HIGH
- October "peak" was discount-manufactured, not organic
- Multi-outlet quality inconsistency as scale outpaced management systems
- Festival season staff turnover compounded operational stress
- Swiggy separately collapsed from Apr 2025 (first crash: -49% in 8 weeks)

### RC-5: Market Saturation 🟡 MEDIUM
- 15+ active competitors vs ~7 in 2022
- 5+ new competitive outlets in BB's primary trade zones since 2023
- DB City QSR hub with no BB presence
- BB made zero strategic adjustments to competitive escalation

### RC-6: Menu Identity Dilution 🟡 MEDIUM
- 20 categories, ~76 estimated SKUs in 200–500 sq ft kitchens
- Only **47.5% revenue from burgers**
- Drift items (pasta, pizza, sandwich) = 11.3% revenue (Rs.10.66L/year)
