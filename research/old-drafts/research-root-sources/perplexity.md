Burger Empire is a fast-food chain specializing in burgers, wraps, fries, pasta, and shakes, primarily operating in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, with a focus on affordable, youth-oriented quick service. Founded in 2018 by Animesh Gupta and Rahul Saraogi from Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, it started with its first company-owned outlet in Gwalior and has expanded to at least six outlets by late 2024. [linkedin](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rahul-saraogi-88997530)

## Brand Overview
Burger Empire positions itself as a budget-friendly alternative to national chains like McDonald's and Burger King, emphasizing fresh ingredients, unmatched taste, and pocket-friendly pricing in a fun ambiance for hanging out with friends. The legal entity appears tied to the founders' operations without public franchise listings; all known outlets are company-owned in Gwalior, with no confirmed ghost or cloud kitchens, though delivery-heavy operations suggest potential dark kitchen use. Ownership is by co-founders Animesh Gupta and Rahul Saraogi, with a corporate structure centered in Gwalior (HQ: City Center, Patel Nagar). [burgerempire](https://burgerempire.in/?page_id=224184)
