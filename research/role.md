## FULL JOB DESCRIPTION

---

### Business Intelligence & Growth Manager
**Burger Empire (A1 Foods) | Gwalior + Remote**
**Reports directly to: Founder (Animesh Gupta)**

---

### Role Purpose

To be the founder's eyes and ears across all platforms, channels, and competitive signals — ensuring that no crisis builds silently, no opportunity is missed, and every key metric is tracked, reported, and acted upon in real time. This is not a content or creative role. This is a monitoring, analysis, and execution role.

---

### Core Responsibilities

#### 1. Platform Health Monitoring
- Track Zomato and Swiggy order counts daily against previous week and previous month baselines
- Monitor Zomato listing visibility, ranking position, and any algorithmic changes
- Manage Zomato Visibility Plan subscription status and performance
- Track ONDC order volumes once registered
- Track WhatsApp Business ordering volumes once launched
- Flag any platform policy changes, commission changes, or fee hikes within 24 hours of announcement
- Maintain a weekly platform health scorecard shared with founder every Monday

#### 2. Review and Reputation Management
- Monitor all incoming reviews across Google, Zomato, Swiggy, Justdial, and Magicpin daily
- Ensure every negative review receives a professional response within 24 hours
- Maintain a review response log — platform, date, rating, issue, response sent
- Track overall rating trends per outlet monthly
- Escalate any review that signals a legal, safety, or theft risk immediately

#### 3. Social Media Intelligence (Not Content Creation)
- Monitor Instagram and Facebook organic vs paid view ratio weekly
- Track content interaction rates and flag when paid spend is not converting
- Report on follower growth trends monthly
- Identify when ad spend is producing no organic lift and recommend stopping or redirecting
- Monitor competitor social media for new offers, campaigns, or outlet announcements

#### 4. Competitor Intelligence
- Track all Gwalior competitor listings on Zomato, Swiggy, and Justdial weekly
- Alert founder within 48 hours if any new outlet opens in a BB trade zone
- Monitor Burger Singh national expansion for any Gwalior entry signal
- Track Burger Budds rating and review trajectory monthly
- Report on any new national QSR Gwalior announcements immediately

#### 5. POS Data Analysis
- Review weekly POS data and prepare a simple order trend summary
- Flag any channel showing more than 15% week-on-week decline immediately
- Track Average Order Value movements monthly
- Monitor discount percentage — alert if exceeds 12% of gross in any month
- Prepare a monthly performance summary across all channels with YoY comparison

#### 6. Monthly Founder Briefing
- Prepare and present a one-page monthly business health report to Animesh
- Include: platform performance, review health, competitor activity, social media ROI, and top 3 decisions needed this month
- Maintain a running action tracker — decisions made, owner, deadline, status

---

### What This Role Does NOT Do
- Does not create content or edit videos — that remains with Kunal
- Does not manage outlet staff or kitchen operations
- Does not run advertising campaigns independently
- Does not make strategic or financial decisions
- Does not replace the founder's judgment — it informs it
